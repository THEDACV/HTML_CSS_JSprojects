Shree Narayana Murthy

Renowned Indian software industrialist and co-founder of Infosys, a multinational corporation.

About the Legend

☛ Narayana Murthy, full name Nagavara Ramarao Narayana Murthy, was born on August 20, 1946, in Mysore, Karnataka, India.

☛ He is the co-founder of Infosys, a global leader in consulting, technology, and outsourcing solutions.

☛ Murthy earned his undergraduate degree in electrical engineering from the National Institute of Engineering, University of Mysore, and his master's degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur.

☛ In 1981, Murthy founded Infosys along with six other software professionals. He served as the CEO from 1981 to 2002 and as the chairman from 2002 to 2011.

☛ Under his leadership, Infosys became one of the leading IT companies in India, known for its innovation, quality, and customer-centric approach.

☛ He introduced the Global Delivery Model, which became the foundation for the Indian IT services industry's growth.

☛ He has been honored with numerous awards, including the Padma Vibhushan and the Padma Shri by the Government of India.

☛ He is also a recipient of the Ernst & Young World Entrepreneur of the Year Award and has been listed among the greatest entrepreneurs of our time by Fortune magazine.

☛ Beyond his professional achievements, Murthy is known for his philanthropy and commitment to social causes.

☛ He has made significant contributions to education, healthcare, and rural development through the Infosys Foundation and other philanthropic initiatives.

☛ Murthy's leadership and vision have not only transformed Infosys into a global powerhouse but also inspired a generation of entrepreneurs and business leaders in India and around the world.
